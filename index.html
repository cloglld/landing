Louis Vuitton short résumé

The Louis Vuitton brand is one of the most iconic and influential luxury brands in the world of fashion and leather goods. Louis Vuitton was founded in 1854 in Paris, France by the eponymous designer and entrepreneur, Louis Vuitton. He opened his first store at 4 rue Neuve-des-Capucines in Paris, which is still the location of the brand's flagship store. Louis Vuitton is a brand that embodies luxury, craftsmanship and innovation. It continues to be an essential reference in the world of fashion and remains faithful to its heritage while evolving with contemporary trends.
